Forms

Standard form $$ ax + by = c $$
Slope-Intercept $$ y = m x + b$$
Point-Slope $$y-y_1 = m(x-x_1)$$
Intercepts $$\frac{x}{a} + \frac{y}{b} = 1$$

From 2 Points to Standard Form

Given two points \( (x_1, y_1) \) and \((x_2, y_2)\), the standard form is found by

$$a=y_2-y_1 \quad b=x1-x2 \quad c=ax_1 + by_1 $$

Intersection of 2 Lines

Given two lines in standard form

$$a_1x + b_1y = c_1 $$ $$a_2x + b_2y = c_2 $$

the intersection point is

$$x = \frac{c_1b_2 - c_2b_1}{a_1b_2-a_2b_1} $$ $$y = \frac{c_1a_2 - c_2a_1}{a_2b_1 - a_1b_2}$$
