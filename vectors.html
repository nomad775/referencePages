Vectors, Etc

Dot Product

$$\textbf{A} \cdot \textbf{B} = \vert{\textbf{A}}\vert{\textbf{B}}\vert\cos{\theta}$$ $$\textbf{A} \cdot \textbf{B} = a_x \cdot b_x \cdot c_x + a_y \cdot b_y \cdot c_y + a_z \cdot b_z \cdot c_z$$

Cross Product

$$A\times B=\vert{A}\vert\vert{B}\vert \sin{\theta} \hat{\textbf{n}} \quad\text{ or as a determinate}\quad \textbf{A} \times \textbf{B} = \det\begin{bmatrix}i & j & k\\ax & ay & az\\bx&by&bz\end{bmatrix}$$ or by components $$\begin{align*}c_x = a_y b_z - a_zb_y \\ c_y=a_zb_x-a_xb_z \\ c_z=a_xb_y-a_yb_x\end{align*}$$

Coordinate system rotations

$$x'= x\cos{\theta} + y\sin{\theta}$$ $$y' = y\cos{\theta} - x\sin{\theta}$$

In matrix form

$$\begin{bmatrix} x' \\ y'\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \cos{\theta} & \sin{\theta} \\ -\sin{\theta} & \cos{\theta} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix}$$

Expanded to 3D

$$R_z(\theta)=\begin{bmatrix} x' \\ y' \\z' \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \cos{\theta} & \sin{\theta} & 0\\ -\sin{\theta} & \cos{\theta} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\z\end{bmatrix}$$

About the X axis

$$R_x(\theta)=\begin{bmatrix} x' \\ y' \\z' \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \cos{\theta} & \sin{\theta} \\ 0 &-\sin{\theta} & \cos{\theta} \\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\z\end{bmatrix}$$

About the Y axis

$$R_y(\theta)=\begin{bmatrix} x' \\ y' \\z' \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \cos{\theta} & 0 & -\sin{\theta} \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ \sin{\theta} & 0 & \cos{\theta} \\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\z\end{bmatrix}$$

Rotating a vector is equivalent to rotating the coordinate system in the opposite direction. Since cos -x = cos x but sin -x = -sin x then the result is chnaging the signs of the sin terms.

SVG User transform

From the SVG spec the user transform, from viewbox to viewport, $U$ is made up of

$$U = UP + US + UR$$ where
  • UP is pan by SVG.currentTranslate
  • US is scale by SVG.currentScale
  • UR is rotate by SVG.currentRotate
